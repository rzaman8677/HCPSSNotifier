What is HCPSSNotifier.com?

HCPSSNotifier is a web-based platform that allows Howard County Public School System (HCPSS) students to stay up-to-date with important events and information related to their school and academic life. By signing up for the service, students can receive notifications about events, assignments, deadlines, and other important information via email or text message. This can be especially useful for students who are busy with extracurricular activities or have a hectic schedule, as it helps them stay organized and ensures that they don't miss out on important events or opportunities.

We provide notifications for nearly everything, such as assignment due dates, special events (concerts, sports games, etc), and more.
